The Challenge of Concentrated Wealth

Holding a large, highly appreciated stock position presents a unique financial dilemma. How do you diversify without incurring significant immediate tax liabilities?

This section provides a powerful tool to help you navigate two common diversification strategies:

  • Tax-Deferred Exchange Funds: These funds allow you to diversify your portfolio while deferring capital gains taxes. However, they typically come with ongoing costs, primarily in the form of high annual expense ratios.
  • Sell and Reinvest: This straightforward approach involves selling your shares, paying the capital gains tax upfront, and then reinvesting the net proceeds into a diversified portfolio of your choosing.

Our calculator helps you objectively compare these paths, revealing which strategy may be more advantageous for your specific situation.
