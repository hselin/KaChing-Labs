The Challenge of Concentrated Wealth

Do you hold a large, highly appreciated stock position? How do you diversify without incurring significant immediate tax liabilities?

This tool helps you navigate two common strategies:

  • Tax-Deferred Exchange Funds: Diversify while deferring taxes, but be aware of high annual fees.
  • Sell and Reinvest: Pay capital gains tax upfront, then reinvest in a portfolio of your choice.

Our calculator finds the most advantageous path and computes your personal Break-Even Expense Ratio. This is the maximum annual fee you should ever pay for an exchange fund, giving you a single, clear number to make a confident decision.
